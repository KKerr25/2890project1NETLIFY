Frequently Asked Questions

How is Cardeal different from other online car buyers?

Cardeal will buy "any" car—even if it has a blown engine. We offer the highest value, quick payment, convenience, and a secure transaction. If you sell your car to us, you aren’t obligated to buy your next one, though our great deals speak for themselves.

How is the price of my car calculated?

Prices are calculated based on the current market value of your car and its condition.

What costs will I be responsible for?

Cardeal is a free service. There are no charges for evaluations and offers. When you sell a car with Cardeal, we cover pick up and towing—no hidden costs.

How quickly can you have my vehicle picked up?

With branches in every state, we can usually pick up your vehicle within 24 to 48 hours after you accept our offer.

What type of documentation will I need to provide?

When filling out our online form, you'll be asked to provide your Vehicle Identification Number (VIN), which includes details about the year, make, model, trim, and more. A valid, signed title in the seller’s name is also required.

Will you buy my car if I can't get it started?

If your car is damaged, wrecked, or non-running, we can still provide a fair market offer tailored to your vehicle’s condition and local market demand.
